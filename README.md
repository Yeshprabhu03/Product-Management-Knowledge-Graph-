## 🏗️ Architecture
The vault is structured across 15 core domains, each containing high-signal notes, real-world case studies, and cross-functional frameworks.

1.  **Strategy & Vision**: Vision, Strategy, North Star, Business Models.
2.  **Research & Discovery**: Continuous Discovery, User Interviews, JTBD.
3.  **AI & ML Fundamentals**: LLMs, RAG, Fine-tuning, Hallucination, Embeddings.
4.  **Data Strategy**: Data Moats, Pipelines, Quality, Privacy.
5.  **Prioritization & Roadmapping**: RICE, Kano, MoSCoW, Trade-offs.
6.  **Execution & Delivery**: AI PRDs, Agile for ML, Tech Debt, Fallbacks.
7.  **Metrics & Analytics**: AARRR, Aha Moment, AI Metrics, Trust Metrics.
8.  **Responsible AI**: Bias, Fairness, Safety, EU AI Act, NIST RMF.
9.  **AI UX Patterns**: Copilot vs Autopilot, Reasoning Disclosure, Graceful Degradation.
10. **GTM & Growth**: Positioning, Pricing, Cost-to-Serve, Growth Loops.
11. **Technical PM**: Build vs Buy, Vendor Evaluation, LLM Architecture.
12. **Leadership & Stakeholders**: Managing Up, ML Eng Collaboration, Cross-functional Alignment.
13. **Frameworks & Models**: OST, Hook Model, Platform Thinking, Flywheels.
14. **Tools & Artifacts**: Model Cards, Datasheets, Red Team Reports.
15. **Career**: AI PM Skills, Interview Prep, Personal Branding.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **Obsidian**: For graph-based knowledge management.
- **Quartz**: For hosting the knowledge graph as a lightning-fast web experience.
- **Tailored CSS**: Optimized for technical readability and aesthetic depth.
